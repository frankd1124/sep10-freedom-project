Massage Gun

The Massage Gun is a handheld device that uses rapid vibration movements in order to provide a deep tissue massage and to relax the muscles. The sole purpose id to relax tight muscles; many athletes use this product after a workout to relax their overworked tissues.

Cryotherapy

Cryotherapy which is usually in a chamber specializes in exposing the body to extremely cold temperatures for a short period of time. The individual remains inside the chamber for a brief session. During there time inside the chamber, the cold temperature triggers a response in the body leading to some reactions such as reducing soreness, helps with pain/soreness, etc. Many athletes use cryotherapy after an intense preformance to recover.
